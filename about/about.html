Functions and Objectives of Samrat Ashok Lord Buddha Welfare Foundation

1. To provide employment to the youth through the organization and to make the youth self-dependent.
2. Free medical camps.
3. To provide free coaching for poor children.
4. To organize awareness programs under Swachh Bharat Swasth Bharat Mission.
5. To provide marriage programs and money to the daughters of poor families.
6. Purchase of land and construction of office for the organization.
7. To give their rights to the dalits, the backward, the most backward and the poor.
8. Organizing special programs on health, medicine, education, employment.
9. Collecting data of young men and women for marriage and conducting an introduction conference.
10. To honor the meritorious students on behalf of the institution.
11. To honor the officers, social workers, and eminent businessmen who have done excellent work for the society.
12. To make people aware to stop the evils spread in the society like alcohol consumption, child labour, child marriage, dowry system, feticide etc.
13. Organizing special awareness programs on environmental protection.
14. To save the environment on a large scale do tree plantation.
15. To demand the Government of India to provide special security arrangements and make strong laws for the protection of women and sisters.
16.To put pressure on the Government of India to enlarge the lot of Emperor Ashoka on Indian notes.
