Food Feeding

Food is the third most basic thing for life after air and water. This demonstrates the significance of food for living.
Food by and large comprises of sugars, proteins, fats as real parts. What's more, minerals, nutrients and follow components as minor segments. These segments are helpful professionally body to support well-being. This food we eat is as grains, beats, natural products, vegetables, oils, meat, and so on.
In spite of the fact that we realize the food is significant and expend it in day by day life, its different jobs in the body are very fascinating and worth perusing.
“When we give food to the poor, they call us a saint. When we ask why the poor have no food, they call us a communist.”
